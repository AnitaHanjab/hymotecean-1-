COMPANY PROFILE

Hymetocean Peers Company (HPC) is a Philippine-based general partnership specializing in technical services related to lakes and coastal ocean studies, water resource management, renewable energy exploration, and both water and air quality modeling. With a strong commitment to environmental sustainability and scientific innovation, HPC delivers reliable solutions for complex environmental challenges. What is HPC?

Hymetocean Peers Company is a response to a consulting need in Coastal Engineering and
Quality Studies using accurate modeling and monitoring tools.

Mission

The company’s mission is to determine the viability and associated environmental impacts and risks of development projects through state-of-the-art computational modeling and innovative environmental monitoring systems.

Vision

Its vision is to be a leading provider of real solutions to environmental and engineering problems through accurate quantification and prediction of project impacts in the water and air environment.

Believes

HPC believes that engineering development and environmental protection can go hand in hand through accurate project impact assessment. In addition, we provide exploration companies the needed analysis for the viability of renewable energy projects including solar, wind, tidal and wave power development.

Capabilities and Expertise

Our technical expertise is highly specialized yet diverse. Above all, we value accuracy and excellence in our work. The consultancy services we offer to research organizations, industry and plant owners, as well as contractors and developers, are designed to address complex environmental and engineering challenges. These services are grouped into four main categories, each reflecting our core strengths and areas of specialization:

Environment & Engineering Studies

This study covers coastal/lake hydrodynamics, oceanography, and coastal engineering; hydrology, hydraulic engineering, and flood studies; sedimentation and siltation in lakes and coastal seas; water quality; hydrogeology and groundwater contamination; meteorology and climate assessment; air and noise quality; and overall environmental and risk assessment.

Computational Support for Rebewable Energy Development

This study also explores renewable energy sources such as tidal energy, wave energy, offshore wind energy, and solar energy

Computational Modeling

This study includes coastal and lake modeling (currents, wave disturbances, surges), sediment transport and erosion, shoreline changes, water and wastewater pollution, thermal plume dispersion, flood and runoff modeling, reservoir sedimentation, salinity intrusion, and air and noise quality with weather prediction.

Ocean Weather Prediction and Hazard Analysis

This study also covers coastal infrastructure planning (dredging, reclamation, breakwaters, ports, and harbors), offshore exploration and met-ocean surveys, tsunami wave inception and inundation, and large-scale sedimentation and erosion modeling including sand accumulation.

We have provided technical services to a number of clients both in the government and the private sectors in and out of the country. Our clients include Dome International, Global Estates Resort Inc., Makati Development Corporation, SM Development Corporation, Nido Petroleum and JG Summit Petrochemical Corporation.

Consulting Fellows and Staff

Our consultants are mainly scientists and engineers who had been educated beyond the post graduate level and gained considerable experience in various consultancy works in environment and engineering. As founder, the present manager (Dr. Paul C. Rivera) is the principal consultant. Some of our consultants come from the academe who are called upon to work for specific projects. Most of our consultants received training abroad with master’s and doctorate degrees.
